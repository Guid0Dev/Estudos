Simple Omellete Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetable, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparaion: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredientes

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetable, cooked meats, herbs

instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: in a bow, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a flutter texture.
  2. heat the pan:Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the buffer is melgfed and bububbling, pour in the edds. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings(optional): when the eggs begin to set at the edges butt are still slightly runny in the middle, prinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and field it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
